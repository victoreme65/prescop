# **App Name**: Prescop

## Core Features:

- User Authentication & Roles: Secure customer and seller registration and login with JWT authentication, ensuring role-based access for customers and sellers. Customers are assigned by default.
- Seller Onboarding & Approval: Process applications for new sellers including business details and bank information, requiring admin approval before a seller can list products.
- Product Management (Seller Dashboard): Sellers can add new products with images, descriptions, categories, price, and stock, as well as view, edit, or delete existing listings.
- AI Product Description Tool: Assist sellers by suggesting enhanced product descriptions based on input product features and keywords.
- Product Browsing & Detail Pages: Customers can view product listings, filter by category, search for specific products, and see detailed product information.
- Shopping Cart & Paystack Checkout: Customers can add/remove products, update quantities, and proceed to a secure checkout with Paystack integration for payment processing.
- Order Tracking & History: Customers can view their purchase history, and sellers can track orders related to their products, with status updates (processing, shipped, delivered, paid/pending).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A warm, sophisticated rose pink (#E0859C) to evoke a sense of beauty and care.
- Background color: A very subtle, desaturated pinkish off-white (#F4F0F1) to provide a clean and elegant canvas.
- Accent color: A soft, muted plum (#CC66B3) for interactive elements and highlights, adding depth without overpowering the main palette.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' (humanist sans-serif) for its elegant and modern aesthetic, aligning with the beauty and fashion industry. Body font: 'Alegreya' (humanist serif) for enhanced readability in product descriptions and reviews, providing an intellectual and contemporary feel.
- Utilize modern, minimalist line icons that align with a premium beauty aesthetic. Icons should be easily discernible and used consistently throughout the platform, especially for navigation and actions.
- Implement a fully responsive, mobile-first design focusing on intuitive navigation. Product cards should be clean and image-focused, while seller and admin dashboards should feature a clear sidebar layout for efficient access to features.
- Incorporate subtle micro-interactions for feedback on user actions, such as adding to cart or submitting a form, and smooth transitions for page loads or state changes to enhance user experience without distraction.
